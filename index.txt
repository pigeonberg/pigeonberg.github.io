- [001] INTRO
_______________________________________________________________________________

My name is Joseph, though I go by various aliases online. This site is a place
for me to collect my thoughts in one spot and stay independent of other
platforms.

It takes a man with a fair bit of ego to speak online as if anyone genuinely
cares what he has to say, but that is not the aim here. This website exists
purely for my own enjoyment, without worrying about SEO or whether anyone else
appreciates it.
